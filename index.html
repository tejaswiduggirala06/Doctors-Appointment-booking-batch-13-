Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by searching for a doctor or specialty, selecting your preferred doctor, choosing an available time slot, and confirming your appointment. You'll receive a confirmation via email and SMS.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment up to 12 hours before the scheduled time. Log in to your account, go to 'My Appointments', and select the option to reschedule or cancel.

Do you offer emergency services?

While we help you connect with doctors quickly, we don't provide emergency services. In case of a medical emergency, please call your local emergency number or go to the nearest hospital.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and medical information. Your data is never shared without your consent.

Common Questions

How do I prepare for my appointment?

Bring any relevant medical records, a list of current medications, and be prepared to discuss your symptoms and medical history in detail.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI payments, and select digital wallets. Some clinics may also accept cash payments.

Do you accept insurance?

Many of our partner clinics accept major insurance providers. Please check with the specific clinic when booking your appointment.
